About Us

The Russian University of Transport (MIIT) Auditorium Booking System is a modern platform designed to streamline the process of reserving classrooms for teachers and staff.

Our system provides a convenient way to find and book available auditoriums based on your specific requirements, including capacity, equipment, and time slots.

With automatic approval for available rooms and instant PDF confirmation generation, booking a classroom has never been easier.

Frequently Asked Questions

To book an auditorium, log in to your account, browse available rooms, select your preferred date and time slot, and submit your booking request. Approved bookings generate an instant PDF confirmation.

Yes, you can cancel your booking before the scheduled time. Go to "My Bookings" and click the cancel button next to the booking you wish to cancel.

Use the filter options on the rooms page to search by building, floor, and equipment type (computers, projector, whiteboard). You can also use the search bar to find rooms by number.

Bookings follow the university schedule with 8 class periods: 8:30-9:50, 10:05-11:25, 11:40-13:00, 13:45-15:05, 15:20-16:40, 16:55-18:15, 18:30-19:50, and 20:00-21:20.

Once your booking is approved, you can download the PDF confirmation from the booking confirmation page or from "My Bookings" by clicking the download button.
